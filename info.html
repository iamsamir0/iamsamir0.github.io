Server Rules & Guidelines

Follow these rules to ensure a fair and enjoyable experience for everyone:

  • No Griefing: Respect other players' builds. Griefing is strictly prohibited.
  • No Cheating: Hacking, using unauthorized mods, or exploiting game glitches is forbidden.
  • Respect Others: Treat all players with respect. No harassment or bullying.
  • No Spamming: Avoid spamming chat with unnecessary messages or caps.
  • No Advertising: Advertising other servers or services in chat is not allowed.
  • Follow Staff Instructions: Obey the rules and listen to the staff. They are here to ensure everyone has a great experience.

How to Join

Follow these steps to join our server:

  1. Connecting to the Server:
    • Open Minecraft PE 0.15.10 and navigate to "Play" -> "Servers."
    • Tap "Add Server" and input the following details:
    • Server Name: LightLand
    • Server Address: your.server.ip
    • Port: default port
    • Tap "Save" and then tap on the server to connect.
  2. Setting Up Your Account:
    • Upon your first login, you may be prompted to register using the `/register` command. Follow the on-screen instructions.
    • If your account needs a password, create a secure one and remember it. Use the `/login` command to log in during future sessions.
